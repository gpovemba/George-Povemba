30-Day Discipline Challenge

You already know your system. This is where you prove you can follow it.

Most trading losses aren't a bad strategy — they're a broken rule. One extra trade, one oversized position, one revenge entry after a red day. The Session locks in your own rules for 30 straight days and keeps an honest record of whether you followed them.

01

Set your rules

Pick your session window, size limit, and the setup you'll trade. You define the line — the app just checks whether you stayed on your side of it.

02

Log every day

One entry per trading day. Timing and size are checked automatically. Setup match and emotional discipline, you call honestly.

03

One break ends it

No partial credit. Break a rule and the run is over — restart when you're ready. Finish clean and you earn a shareable certificate.
